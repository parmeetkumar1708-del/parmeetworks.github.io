HIRAGANA CHART

Hinglish: Hiragana = Japanese ka "ABCD". 46 characters, har ek ek sound. Hindi mein क = ka sound, waise hi か = ka. Tap karke pronunciation suno!

PARTICLES — GRAMMAR CORE

Hinglish: Particles = words ke baad aane wale tiny words jo role batate hain. Hindi mein "ko, se, mein, ne" jaisa. N5 ka dil hain yeh!
は
WA — TOPIC MARKER
Sentence ka topic highlight karta hai
Hindi mein "Main [topic] hoon" — "wa" wahi kaam karta hai. "Watashi WA" = "MERA topic ye hai"!
わたし は がくせい です。Watashi wa gakusei desu.Main student hoon.
これ は ほん です。Kore wa hon desu.Yeh ek kitaab hai.
を
WO — OBJECT MARKER
Jo cheez action receive kare
りんご を たべます。Ringo wo tabemasu.Seb khata hoon. (wo = "ko")
みず を のみます。Mizu wo nomimasu.Paani peeta hoon.
に
NI — DIRECTION + TIME
Kahan ja rahe + kab
がっこう に いきます。Gakkou ni ikimasu.School jaata hoon.
しちじ に おきます。Shichiji ni okimasu.7 baje uthta hoon.
で
DE — LOCATION OF ACTION
Kahan kaam ho raha hai
がっこう で べんきょうします。Gakkou de benkyou shimasu.School MEIN padhta hoon.

NUMBERS — 1 TO 10

Hinglish: Numbers bahut logical hain! 11 = 10+1 = じゅういち. 23 = にじゅうさん. Tap karke pronunciation suno!

VERBS — BASIC FORMS

Hinglish: Japanese verbs sentence ke AAKHIR mein aate hain — Hindi jaisa! "Main khata hoon" = "Watashi wa TABEMASU". ます (masu) = polite present.
